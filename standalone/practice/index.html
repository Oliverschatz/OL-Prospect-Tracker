PMI-RMP Seminar Pack

A pack of practice questions for in-class discussion.

You will answer 20 questions in sequence. There is no feedback during the pack — correct answers and rationales are revealed only after you submit, so the class can review them together.

Tools available during the pack:

  • Strike-through — click the × on any option to mark it as eliminated.
  • Flag for review — flag questions to revisit before submitting.
  • Calculator and Notepad — open from the top bar.
  • Timer — counts up; included in your results.

You can navigate freely between questions using Prev/Next or the question grid on the right.
